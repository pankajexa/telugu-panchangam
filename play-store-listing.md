## Full Description (4000 char max)

మనCalendar — your daily companion for authentic Telugu panchangam, Dharma Shastra-backed festival practices, and sacred Ramayana shlokas.

Built for the Telugu-speaking community worldwide, మనCalendar brings the wisdom of traditional Hindu timekeeping to your phone with precise astronomical calculations for Hyderabad, Vijayawada, Visakhapatnam, and other cities.

📅 DAILY PANCHANGAM
• Tithi, Nakshatra, Yogam, Karanam with exact transition times
• Sunrise, sunset, and Rahu Kalam timings
• Moon phase visualization with Paksha tracking
• Expandable cards showing multi-day transition spans
• Telugu and English language support

🗓️ CALENDAR
• Switch between English months and Telugu Hindu months (Chaitram, Vaishaakham, etc.)
• Color-coded Hindu month boundaries
• Purnima and Amavasya moon badges on calendar days
• Year view for quick navigation
• Tap any date to see full panchangam details

🎉 FESTIVALS & VRATAS
• All major Telugu festivals: Ugadi, Sri Rama Navami, Vinayaka Chavithi, Dussehra, Deepavali, Sankranti, and more
• Ekadashi, Sankashtahara Chaturthi, and Pradosham vratas
• Dharma Shastra-backed practices — what to do, mantras to chant, puja vidhi, and food guidelines for each festival
• Filter by upcoming, major festivals, or vratas

⏰ MUHURTA
• 24-hour muhurta timeline visualization
• Abhijit Muhurta, Brahma Muhurta, and other auspicious windows
• Rahu Kalam, Yamagandam, and Gulika Kalam inauspicious periods
• Plan your important activities around auspicious timings

📿 RAMAYANA SHLOKAS
• 19 sacred shlokas from Valmiki Ramayana
• Sanskrit in Devanagari and Telugu script
• IAST transliteration for proper pronunciation
• Telugu and English meanings
• Shastra backing explaining the significance of each shloka
• Browse by category: Protection, Devotion, Courage, Peace, and more

📤 SHARE CENTER
• Share beautiful daily panchangam cards with family on WhatsApp
• Share Ramayana shlokas as stunning image cards
• Festival-specific sharing with deity images and wishes
• Special Sri Rama Navami mode with curated Rama shlokas and Pattabhishekam artwork

🔔 SMART NOTIFICATIONS
• Daily Sandhya Vandana alarms synced to actual sunrise and sunset
• Never miss Brahmi Muhurta, Pratar Sandhya, or Sayam Sandhya

🌍 LOCATION-AWARE
• Accurate calculations based on your city's coordinates
• Support for major Indian cities with precise latitude/longitude
• Custom location support

🎨 BEAUTIFUL DESIGN
• Modern, clean interface inspired by traditional aesthetics
• Hindu iconography: Om, Diya, Kalash, Lotus, Trishul
• Smooth animations and transitions
• Dark navy and saffron gold color palette
• Works offline — no internet required for daily panchangam

మనCalendar is made with devotion for our Telugu community. All festival practices and shloka selections are grounded in Dharma Shastra and respected Hindu institutions.
